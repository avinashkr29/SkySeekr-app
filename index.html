Your Privacy Matters

At SkySeekr, we take your privacy seriously. Here's what you need to know about how we handle your data:

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality, including:

Your data is used solely for providing AR features and improving app functionality. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Protection
